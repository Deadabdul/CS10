Mac n Cheese Recipe!

Learn how to make a Mac n Cheese with Kraft Dinner (microwave)

Ingredients

  • a pack of 225g Kraft Dinner
  • 6 cup bowl
  • 3/4 cup of hot water
  • non-hydrogenated margarine
  • 1/3 cup skim milk

Instructions

  1. Add pasta into 6-cup microwaveable bowl.
  2. Add 1-3/4 cups hot water, stir.
  3. Microwave on high 8 to 9 min, or until water is absorbed stirring every 3 min.
  4. Add 1 tbsp. non-hydrogenated margarine, 1/3 cup skim milk and the cheese sauce mix
  5. Mix well and enjoy!
